Why toggle scales?

Linear + Linear shows the explosive recent growth in raw dollar terms â€” early years are flattened near zero.
Linear + Log (price) spreads out the early history so you can see every cycle clearly.
Log + Log is the classic power-law view: if BTC follows a power law, price becomes a straight line on a log-log chart.
Log time + Linear price compresses the early quiet years while keeping dollar moves intuitive.
